Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 15, 2025

Thank you for using Smart Clock. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

Smart Clock does not collect any personal or sensitive user data.

However, the app may request permission to:

  • Display over other apps – to ensure alarms function correctly even when using other applications.
  • Send notifications – to trigger alarms, timers, and reminders.

2. How We Use Permissions

All permissions are strictly used to enable the app’s core features:

  • Overlay permission: Display alarm alerts over other running apps.
  • Notification access: Send scheduled alarms and timer alerts.

We do not:

  • Collect or share personal data
  • Use analytics or third-party tracking
  • Access your location, contacts, or media

3. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years of age. If we discover such data has been collected, we will delete it immediately.

4. Security

We use local device storage and standard safeguards to protect your data and app settings. No information is transmitted off the device.

5. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be reflected here with a new effective date.
